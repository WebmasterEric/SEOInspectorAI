How Google Evaluates Pages

Before backlinks and authority, Google asks a simple question: “Does this page clearly solve the query?”

The evaluation order

  1. Topic clarity
  2. Intent match
  3. Content depth
  4. Structural consistency
  5. Trust signals

Why structure comes first

A page without structural clarity cannot earn trust — no matter how many links it has.
