Why most SEO audits fail

Tools surface hundreds of “issues” but rarely tell you what to fix first. Rankings are lost due to prioritization mistakes and unclear intent — not missing checkboxes.

Fix priority ladder

  1. High impact: Indexing, intent mismatch, page structure, conversion path
  2. Medium impact: Internal linking, topical support pages, trust signals
  3. Low impact: Cosmetic tweaks

The checklist

  • Intent: Does the page match what the searcher expects?
  • Structure: Can a human understand the page in 10 seconds?
  • Indexing clarity: Clean canonicals, stable URLs, no accidental blocking
  • Internal authority: Do related pages support this topic?
  • Trust: Is it obvious who wrote it and why it’s credible?
  • Conversion: Is there a clear next step that makes sense?
