Top local SEO mistakes

  • No real service pages: One generic page trying to rank for everything.
  • Weak location signals: No clear service area, no city intent support.
  • Inconsistent NAP: Name/address/phone mismatch across listings.
  • No internal linking: Pages exist but don’t support each other.
  • Trust missing: No proof, no story, no reason to believe.

What to fix first

Start with clarity: a clean service page that matches the query, a strong conversion path, and internal links that confirm topical relevance.
